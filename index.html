Mission

PubliMentor exists to advance science by making academic publishing clearer, fairer, and more robust. We bring hands‑on editorial experience and deep knowledge of COPE guidance to help communities publish trustworthy research at pace — without compromising integrity.

  • Practical, real‑world solutions
  • Ethics‑first, author‑positive approach
  • Capacity building for lasting impact

Who we help

  • Early‑career and senior researchers preparing submissions
  • Peer reviewers seeking confidence and structure
  • Journal teams facing complex ethics questions
  • New editors building effective, diverse boards
